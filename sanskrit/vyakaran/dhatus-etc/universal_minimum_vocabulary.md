# Universal Minimum Vocabulary

---

## VERBS

### Tier 1 — Semantic Primitives
*These ~15 verbs are the irreducible core identified by Anna Wierzbicka's Natural Semantic Metalanguage research. Any other verb can be paraphrased using combinations of these.*

be, do, happen, move, have, say, think, know, want, feel, see, hear, live, die, become

---

### Tier 2 — Complete Practical Set
*~150 verbs that, combined with nouns, can express any action without awkward circumlocution.*

**Motion**
go, come, run, walk, fly, swim, fall, rise, climb, crawl, jump, enter, leave, follow, chase, approach, retreat, turn, pass, cross, reach, return, escape

**Transfer & Handling**
give, take, bring, send, carry, drag, throw, catch, hold, drop, put, place, lift, pour, fill, empty, push, pull, load, unload, gather, spread, mix, separate

**Physical Action on Objects**
hit, strike, cut, break, tear, bend, twist, squeeze, press, rub, scratch, dig, build, make, destroy, open, close, tie, untie, hang, attach, remove, cover, uncover, sharpen, grind, weave, sew, cook, burn, wash, clean, polish, shape, carve

**Body & Biological**
eat, drink, breathe, sleep, wake, rest, grow, heal, hurt, bleed, sweat, cry, laugh, cough, sneeze, spit, bite, chew, swallow, kiss, touch, born, age, decay

**Perception**
see, look, hear, listen, feel, smell, taste, notice, find, search, watch

**Mind & Emotion**
think, know, believe, remember, forget, understand, learn, teach, decide, choose, want, need, fear, love, hate, like, enjoy, suffer, hope, dream, imagine, plan, doubt, agree, refuse, count, compare, judge

**Speech & Communication**
say, speak, ask, answer, call, name, tell, sing, shout, whisper, promise, warn, describe, read, write, signal

**Social & Existential**
exist, stay, start, stop, continue, meet, help, fight, work, play, trade, marry, own, lose, win, share, hide, show, wait, try, fail, succeed, rule, obey, pray, celebrate, mourn
